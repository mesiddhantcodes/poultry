Hariom Feeds Pvt. Ltd.

Hariom Feeds Pvt. Ltd. The Company Was Established In 2003 By Mr. Dilip Kumar Singh With The Single Poultry Unit In Gopalganj, (Bihar). Now A Day, Hariom Feeds Pvt. Ltd. Is Amongst The Top Market Leaders of The Finest-Quality of Cattle & Poultry Feeds Across India Producing 250 MT Per Day. Hariom Feeds Pvt. Ltd. Soon Diversified Into Various Segments of Poultry Feeds Layer Feeds, (Crumbs & Mash), Broiler Feeds (Hi-Pro & Future), Cattle Feeds (Milk Booster, Premium Power, & Super Shakti) And Also Broiler Integrated Farming in Bihar, Jharkhand & UttarPradesh With Hatcheries . Today Hariom Feeds Pvt. Ltd. Group In Poultry Sector Requirement of More Than 15 States of India With A Network of 500 Dealers And More Than 500 Numbers of Employees, Hariom Feeds Pvt. Ltd. Has Developed Research & Development Wing With In-House Laboratories. Our Staff And Employees Have Extensive Industry Experience And Are Committed To Maintain And Enhance The Quality And Variety of Its Animal Feed & Livestock Products. Within A Short Span of Time, Hariom Feeds Pvt. Ltd. Has Become The Preferred Choice For Farmers In Broiler, Layer, Cattle Feeds, We Are The India's Leading Processing Company For Poultry & Cattle Feeds Catering To The Needs of Various Big Companies, Who Have Taken Processing of Their Feeds In Our Established Plants Equipped With Modern Technique At Various Locations of India.Our Company’s motto is "GROW POULTRY & FARMER FOR HAPPY INDIA".

Hariom Feeds Pvt. Ltd. is a feed manufacturing company producing compounded poultry Feed.
